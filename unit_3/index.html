Paid Social

What is it? Digital Marketing on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn and other social media platforms is available to your business, no matter the size.

Case Study: B2C example

An local automotive parallel importer's objectives for online advertising was clear, to bring leads for the showroom car sales team to convert into purchases. Successful sales leads generation is measured by the number of customers and potential customers who eventually purchase a car as a result of the advertisements on social media (Facebook) in this case. With a budget of a mere ten dollars a day, after a period of 7 days, two cars (a car in Singapore has a price tag of at least ninety thousand dollars) were sold as a result of the campaign. Selection of audience target and timing of advertisements are important. The ability to find "lookalike" audiences, remarketing to "website custom audiences" is available for all business, if your business sets it up correctly at the start of the campaign.

Paid Search

What is it? Customers turn to online sources to research on products and services before they purchase. Be easily found on Google and other search engines through AdWords Advertising.

Case Study: B2C example

A local beauty salon buying keywords on Google Adwords to find new customers who would come by for a trial service. In general, we have observed across industries that google search adwords tend to cost 2 to 3 times per click versus a facebook ad cost per click. That itself should not be a deterrent for google ad campaigns to increase visitors to your business' website because (potential) customers who search for keywords related to your business are searching to purchase or to collect information before purchase. The spend to be invested for google adwords could be calculated as a percentage of potential revenue for successful conversion after X clickthroughs. Timing of advertisements, ad copy (text in ad) testing and competitive analysis are some keys to success.
